Admissions

Required Documents for Admission

  • 1. Birth Certificate (Original and Photocopy)
  • 2. Marksheet of Previous Class (Original and Photocopy)
  • 3. Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • 4. Aadhar Card (Original and Photocopy)
  • 5. Passport-sized Photographs (4 copies)
  • 6. Medical Certificate (if required)
  • 7. Any Other Relevant Documents (please specify)

Other Information

Please ensure that all documents are submitted along with the admission form before the deadline.
